Common Questions

Do you cover my area?

I cover Dublin, Wicklow and Kildare, with no hidden travel fees within that area. If you're just outside it, get in touch, I can often still help.

How much does a measured survey cost?

Prices depend on the size of the property, but a standard 2-3 bedroom, two-storey home survey typically starts from around €350 - €450.

How long does it take, from booking to receiving my drawings?

The site visit itself usually takes 1-2 hours, and you'll have your finished CAD drawings within 2-3 working days.

Do I need to be home during the visit?

Not at all. Many clients prefer to leave a key or arrange access so they can carry on with their day. If you’d prefer to be there to walk me through the property, that’s perfectly fine too.

Can you handle unusual or older properties?

Definitely. Older houses, attic conversions, and homes with irregular layouts are exactly where an accurate survey makes the biggest difference. Whether it’s a period property with thick walls or a house where nothing is perfectly square, I’ll provide the precise, as-built drawings you need to get your renovation off to a solid start.

Can these drawings be used for planning applications?

Yes! Local authorities require accurate existing drawings. By getting these from me first, you save money and can hand a ready-to-go CAD file straight to your architect or planner, speeding up your application.

Do these drawings include structural or planning advice?

No. These surveys are for spatial and planning reference purposes only. They do not constitute a structural report or building assessment; please consult a qualified structural engineer regarding any structural changes or load-bearing concerns.
